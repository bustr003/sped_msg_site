Help: Email Drafts


Set up text blocks to be written around data elements.

Suggested procedure

Step 1: Add data to a Google Sheet.

Step 2: Populate columns in the order that you want the data to appear in the email.
1 qwerty
2 RECIPIENT
3 DATA_1
4 DATA_2
5 DATA_3
6 DATA_4

Step 3: For all rows, set COLUMN 1 to "qwerty"
If less than 4 data elements are needed, populate unneedd columns with "nbsp;"

Step 4: Download data from as a csv.

Step 5: In the SpEd Message Builder, populate the text blocks.
If less than 4 text blocks are needed, the unneeded text blocks can be left blank.

Step 6: The Email Drafts page will generate email drafts. Copy and paste all drafts into a Google Doc.

Step 7: From the Google Doc, paste an email draft into Gmail.

Step 8: Send the email.
