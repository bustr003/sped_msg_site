Help: uu Email Drafts


Email drafts to check in on Unaffirmed IEPs, Unaffirmed Amendments, Unsigned IEPs, or Unsigned Amendments.

Suggested procedure

Step 1: Download data from SEIS as an Excel sheet

Step 2: Adjust columns
1 qwerty
2 id
3 fname
4 lname
5 case manager
6 meeting date

Step 3: For all rows, set COLUMN 1 to "qwerty"

Step 4: Download data from Excel as a csv. Optional: Exclude ROW 1.

Step 5: In the SpEd Message Builder, populate the fields on the uu page.

Step 6: The uu page will generate email drafts. Copy and paste all drafts into a Google Doc.

Step 7: From the Google Doc, paste an email draft into Gmail.

Step 8: Send the email.
